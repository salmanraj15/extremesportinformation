J.D GIBBS

Since October of 1997, J.D. Gibbs has served as president of Joe Gibbs Racing. Under Gibbs, the team has expanded from a one-car operation, to a three car team with the #18 M&Ms Toyota Camry driven by Kyle Busch, the #20 Home Depot Camry driven by Joey Logano, and the #11 FedEx Camry, driven by Denny Hamlin. The team also fields Nationwide Series entries for all three drivers.
