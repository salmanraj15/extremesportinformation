KAYAKING TRAINING

1:Always wear a DOT-compliant helmet, goggles, long sleeves, long pants, over-the-ankle boots, and gloves
2: Except for dual-purpose models, never paddle on dangerous surfaces except to cross when done safely and permitted by law
3: Never paddle under the influence of alcohol or drugs
4: Supervise paddlers younger than 16
5: Never permit youngsters to paddle kayaks that are too tall or too powerful for their capabilities
6: Paddle in the company of others so you can assist each other in the event of trouble
7: Paddle only on designated trails and at a safe location
8: Take a hands-on riding course
